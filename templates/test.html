About

Version 1 of Inertia City sample uses textbook-like learning rather than interactive simulations and minigames as the user progresses through the world. The hypothosis is that results from Version 1 user retained knowledge survey will validate the need for gameplay and interaction in learning, rather than just optional text reading, in order to be successful.

Instructions

Enter anonymous, easy-to-remember user id so that if you leave and come back, you can pick up where you left off.

When you walk by a popup and don't want to press "Start," just keep walking and it will go away

Be sure to work on this research survey as you play!!!
